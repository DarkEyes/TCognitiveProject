Loaded packages

Scenario presets

This demonstrator implements tcog-retrieval.md v0.3 mechanical mode. The retrieval engine performs cue matching, negative-cue checks, activation policy enforcement, and constraint triggering — all in JavaScript, all in your browser, all auditable. No backend. No telemetry. Packages load from the local packages/ folder.

Composition into prose is the only step where an LLM is involved. The selected provider is asked to phrase pre-retrieved content according to the layered output format (Section A: cited claims, B: constraints, C: patterns, D: synthesis-marked-as-inference). The LLM is not asked to retrieve, choose clusters, or judge relevance — those decisions are made mechanically.
